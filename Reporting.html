📈 Reporting & Compliance

Facility users can generate reports to monitor case activity, track certifier performance, and ensure timely registration.

📅 Event Summary Report

  • Shows all events by month and facility
  • Includes case counts, status breakdowns, and average completion time

🧑‍⚕️ Death Summary Report

  • Lists all death cases with certifier details
  • Includes status, timestamps, and validation flags
  • Useful for internal audits and DOH reviews

📤 Export Options

  • Export to PDF or Excel
  • Use filters to customize report scope
  • Download directly from the toolbar
