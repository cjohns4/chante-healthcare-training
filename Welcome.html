Welcome to eVitals Training

This training portal provides a comprehensive, role-based guide for licensed Pennsylvania medical facility users navigating the eVitals death reporting system. Whether you're a Data Entry Specialist, Pronouncer, or Certifier, this site supports your workflow with technical guidance, validation logic, and system navigation tips.

What Is eVitals?

  • Electronic registration of death cases
  • Real-time validation against CDC/NCHS standards
  • Role-based workflows for medical facilities, funeral homes, and coroners
  • Secure document uploads and inter-facility case transfers

Who Should Use This Site?

  • External-Death MF Users: Create and manage death cases, assign pronouncers/certifiers, and upload documentation
  • Pronouncers: Affirm time/date/place of death and complete pronouncement tab
  • Certifiers: Certify cause of death and affirm case completion
  • Facility Administrators: Approve user access, manage training compliance, and oversee case queues

What You'll Learn

  • Logging in via Keystone ID and PIN authentication
  • Navigating the dashboard and managing queues
  • Creating and certifying death cases
  • Handling duplicate cases and validation errors
  • Uploading maternal and rare cause documentation
  • Communicating via case messages and comments
  • Generating reports and monitoring compliance
