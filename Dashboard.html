📊 Dashboard Navigation

The eVitals dashboard is your command center. It displays active death cases, system messages, and queue assignments based on your role and facility.

🗂️ Queue Types

  • My Queue: Cases assigned directly to you
  • Facility Queue: All cases linked to your facility
  • Unassigned Queue: Cases awaiting ownership
  • Certification Required: Cases needing certifier action

🎨 Color Indicators

  • Red: Case is 25+ days old
  • Orange: Case is 10–24 days old
  • Green: Case is under 10 days

⚙️ Preferred Views

Customize your dashboard to show only relevant queues. Use filters to sort by urgency, status, or assigned role. This improves efficiency and reduces missed deadlines.
