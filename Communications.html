💬 Case Communication

eVitals supports secure messaging between users, facilities, and roles. Use comments and messages to clarify case details or request action.

📨 Case Messages

  • Send messages to individuals, roles (e.g., Certifier), or facilities
  • Use Functional Entity messaging for broader communication
  • Messages appear in the recipient’s dashboard

🗒️ Comments

  • Internal Comments: Visible only to your facility
  • External Comments: Shared with other facilities or roles
  • All comments are timestamped and stored in the case history

🧾 Audit Trail

All communications are logged. Deleted messages are marked but retained for compliance review.
